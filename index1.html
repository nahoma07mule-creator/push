Why Choose DA Union Bank?

DA Union Bank traces its institutional lineage to the establishment of the Addis Ababa Job Creation Project Office in 1996. This initiative was conceived under the auspices of the Addis Ababa City Administration with the explicit objective of providing financial services to underserved populations and low-income entrepreneurs who lacked access to the formal banking sector.
